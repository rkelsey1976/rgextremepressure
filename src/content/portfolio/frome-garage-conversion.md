A garage conversion in Frome — turning unused space into proper living area.

Cold, damp shell to warm, habitable room. Full damp-proof course and insulated floor slab so it stays dry and warm. Internal wall insulation throughout. New window and door replacing the garage opening — natural light where there was none. Electrics, plumbing, and heating tied straight into the existing system.

Plastered, decorated, finished to match the rest of the property. Looks like it was always there.
