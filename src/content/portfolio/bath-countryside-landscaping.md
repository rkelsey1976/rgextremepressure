A stunning landscaping project in the beautiful Bath countryside.

Every element chosen for a reason — cobble borders for definition, sandstone paving for durability, ACO drainage properly integrated to handle the slope. Water feeds on all four corners so there's always a hose point within reach. Bedded-in irrigation so planting practically maintains itself.

And the greenhouse — sitting on three courses of reclaimed empirical London brick. A touch of history in a contemporary garden.

## Highlights

- Cobble borders adding definition and structure
- Sandstone paving slabs — durable enough for decades
- ACO drainage properly graded and integrated
- Water feed on all four corners
- Bedded-in irrigation system
- Greenhouse on three courses of reclaimed empirical London brick
