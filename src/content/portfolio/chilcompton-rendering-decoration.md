Complete exterior rendering and decoration of a property in Chilcompton.

The original render had deteriorated badly — cracking, weather damage, and years of patch repairs that were no longer holding up. The client needed a full external refresh that would protect the building and look the part.

## What we did

- Full strip-back of existing render
- Re-render using a breathable system suited to the property's construction
- Clean edge details and crisp decorative finish throughout
- Every elevation rendered to a consistent standard

The result: a property that looks like it's just been built — but built right this time.
