## What Was Wrong With the Old Site

The old Wix site had a few problems:

**It was slow.** Google rewards fast websites and penalises slow ones. A slow site doesn't just frustrate visitors — it actively pushes you down the search rankings.

**It only had one or two pages.** When someone searches "driveway cleaning Clifton" or "gutter cleaning Keynsham," Google wants to show them a page that's specifically about that. A one-page site can't compete with businesses that have dedicated pages for every service and every area.

**It didn't show your work properly.** Your before-and-after photos are one of your biggest selling points. People want to see what you can do before they pick up the phone. The old site wasn't making the most of them.

**It didn't tell Google what you do.** There's a way of labelling your website so Google understands exactly what services you offer, where you work and what your reviews say. The old site had none of this in place, which meant your 111 five-star reviews weren't showing up in Google search results.

**It's fast.** The site loads in under a second. This matters because Google uses page speed as a ranking factor, and visitors abandon slow sites quickly.

**It's hosted on a professional platform.** Your site is hosted on Netlify, which is used by millions of businesses worldwide. It's fast, secure and reliable — no downtime, no maintenance, no monthly hosting headaches.

**It's easy to update.** Because everything is built in a structured way, adding a new area page, a new blog post or a new service page is straightforward. The site is built to grow with your business.
